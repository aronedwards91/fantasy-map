Codex Ellium - Terra Orbis - 455 ARB

A world in Flux

The world of Terra Orbis lies in a deep state of turmoil, multiple factions slide between ever changing alliances each trying to ply their hand, via war or diplomacy. Smaller city states hang in the balance trying to placate their masters while isolating themselves from wastefull violence and trouble, the world already providing enough of those itself.

The Factions of Order

The seven civilizations hold the vast quantity of land

  • Nelomica

    A heavily ritualistic race,

  • Dormum

    The most populous & powerful Empire

  • Fierfloc

    A Huge tribal group, at odds amongst each other as often as anything else.

  • Morajiin

    A mix of nomads and rich cities, holds the desert, their trades pass to all lands.

  • Ridokin

    A largely pacifist monastic brotherhood.

  • Kairn

    The brutish, hairy and huge peoples of the cold south.

  • Dwarves

    The short and stocky craftsmen bringing light and beauty to the deep.

The Factions of Chaos

Spread throughout the land these peoples, whilst less populous command no smaller mark upon the movements of history

  • Naga

    Snakebodied fire worshippers

  • Orkkin

    The ferocious nomads of the plains

  • Ghasts

    The ghostly attendants of pain & death

  • Hags

    Accursed denizens of the foulest swamps

  • Elodhiir

    The immortal body capturing men of the ashlands

  • RedFurs

    The pestilent skitterers of the darkest places below

  • Prometheans

    The mysterious and hidden cult of the dark sciences
